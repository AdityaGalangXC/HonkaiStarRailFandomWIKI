Xianzhou Luofu

Xianzhou Luofu, a flagship of the Xianzhou Alliance, sails through the starry expanse, aimed at foes of the Lord of The Hunt. Post-war, it fosters interstellar trade and cultural exchange, aiding worlds affected by conflict. Despite peace, it remains vigilant against threats, with Cloud Knights investigating silent planets and the Xianzhou Ten-Lords Commission guarding against immortality seekers.

Xianzhou Cangcheng

Xianzhou Cangcheng was once the busiest among nine flagship ships that formed the Xianzhou Alliance. It housed the extinct Plum Blossom Cangcheng, now depicted as a glowing blue plant, used as a model for virtual bonsai. Stone Calf Cimicifugae Infusion aimed to soothe excessive teeth growth. In 2609, Cangcheng and others introduced their Yellowjacket Census and Almanacs, marking deaths on Xianzhou. During the Alliance's formation, seven ships convened, creating the Xianzhou Alliance.

Xianzhou Native

The Xianzhou natives originally came from a planet ruled by the Primeval Imperium. A Denizens of Abundance race, the wingweavers, attacked the Primeval Imperium. After defeating the invaders, the Primeval sovereign became fixated on immortality after obtaining miraculous recipes from captured wingweaver magicians. A fleet of nine ships were constructed and sent out into space to search for Yaoshi the Aeon of Abundance in order to obtain immortality

The Enigma of the Immortal Curse

Mara, also known as the "Long-Life Curse," refers to the negative effects that afflict long-life species at a certain point in their life cycle involving cell transformation that result in bodily growths and loss of sanity. Those afflicted by Mara have turned into monsters such as the Mara-Struck Soldier. The cause of mara is not entirely understood but different sources point toward either immortality obtained through Yaoshi The Abundance or the accumulation of memories and the inability to process them over a long period of time. Some individuals affected by mara will exhibit different personalities, and not all of them transform into mara monsters.

Aeon

Veiled in mystery, the Aeons emerge as ethereal, godlike entities beyond mortal comprehension within the realm of Honkai: Star Rail, reigning over the cosmos with enigmatic authority. Each Aeon's inception births a Path, a cryptic course woven into existence, subject to the whims of its celestial creator. These enigmatic beings possess the unfathomable ability to imbue mortals with their divine essence, transforming them into Emanators, conduits of otherworldly power.
